1. About Our App

Photo To Puzzle, developed by Caffeinated Dev ("we," "us," or "our"), is an offline game app that does not collect or store personal data directly. We use the Unity Ads SDK to serve advertisements, which collects certain data to provide personalized ads. In the future, we may add features that require additional data collection, and we will update this policy accordingly. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collected by Unity Ads

The Unity Ads SDK collects the following data to serve ads and improve ad performance:

  • Approximate Location: For ad targeting and analytics.
  • Device Identifiers: For app functionality and ad personalization.
  • Purchase History: For ad targeting and analytics.
  • App Activity: Page views, taps, and usage times during ad interactions (not gameplay) for ad performance and fraud prevention.
  • App Diagnostics: For app functionality and analytics.

Unity Ads does not collect precise location, name, email, financial details, health data, messages, photos, videos, or other sensitive information. All data is encrypted in transit. For details, see Unity’s Privacy Policy.

Our App does not collect additional data, but future features may involve data collection, which will be disclosed in an updated policy.

3. How Data Is Used and Shared

Data collected by Unity Ads is used for:

  • Delivering personalized ads.
  • Analyzing ad performance and user retention.
  • Ensuring app functionality, security, and compliance.

Data may be shared with Unity Ads and their partners for ad services, which may be considered a "sale" under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We may also share data with service providers (e.g., hosting providers) or for legal compliance. For details on Unity Ads’ data practices, see Unity’s Privacy Policy.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You can control your data as follows:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: Adjust device settings (e.g., limit ad tracking on iOS or opt out of ads personalization on Android).
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of data collected by Unity Ads via Unity’s Privacy Policy.

GDPR Rights (EEA Users): If you’re in the EEA, you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your data, object to processing, or withdraw consent for ads. Contact Unity Ads or us at hello.caffeinateddev@gmail.com.

CCPA Rights (California Residents): You can request to know, delete, or opt out of the "sale" of your data (via device settings or Unity Ads). We do not discriminate against users exercising these rights. Contact Unity Ads or us at hello.caffeinateddev@gmail.com.

Data is retained only as long as necessary, per Unity Ads’ policies. For more, see Unity’s Privacy Policy.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not for children under 13 (or 16 in some regions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, contact us at hello.caffeinateddev@gmail.com.
